Environment and Chamber Configuration

Inspection Procedure

Verify the environmental conditions of the installation location meet the listed specifications below and that the unit has been correctly set up. Log the requested outcome information.

Workspace Environment and Unit Setup
Verification Specification Outcome Notes
Ambient: Unit installed indoors, yes or no? Indoor use only. A yes answer indicates the unit is installed indoors.    
Ambient: Measure and record the relative humidity of the workspace. Workspace temperature    
Ambient: Measure and record the relative humidity of the workspace. Not more than 80% at 25°C    
Ambient: Sufficient ventilation space around the unit, yes or no? See the Installation chapter in the user manual for the required spacing.    
Setup: The leveling feet are installed in the unit, yes or no? All four feet or castors are installed.    
Setup: Unit is level, yes or no? The unit is level and stable.    
Setup: The unit interior and shelving have unit interior has been cleaned, yes or no? Cleaned in accordance with user manual Installation Cleaning and Disinfection instructions.    
Setup: Access port stopper installed, yes or no? The port stopper is installed as per the Access Port entry in the Installation chapter of the user manual.*    

 

Acceptance Criteria and Results

Acceptance Criteria Result
Ambient conditions are within the manufacturer specified limits.  
Setup: The unit is configured per the manufacturer specifications.  

*The Access Port cap may be removed and then reinstalled during the OQ to introduce temperature study thermocouple probes into the incubation chamber.
