Restore the Unit to Operational Configuration

After successfully completing the Temperature Distribution study, restore the unit to its operational configuration. Steps noted as such below may be performed to client specifications.

Restore Unit
Action Specifications Outcome
Temperature Study Test Equipment Removed  All test equipment such as thermocouple probes and non-stick tape must be removed from the unit prior to signing off the OQ Completed. Test Equipment Removed?
Yes No
   
Access Port Sealed  

The access port cover or stopper must be in place, as per the Access Port entry in the Installation chapter of the user manual.

-Or-

Any client sensor probes may now be installed in the . However, the access port cover or stopper must make an airtight seal around the probes or any other telemetry wires once the probes are in place. Tape and insulation may be used to seal any gaps.
