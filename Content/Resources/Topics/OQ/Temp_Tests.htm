Vacuum: Controls and Supply System

Evacuate the oven chamber using the pump or building supply system. See the Put the Chamber Under Vacuum procedures in the Operation chapter of the user manual.

  • Verify the Vent and Vacuum valves are in the correct positions.
  • The pump or system must obtain at least 125 Torr (-25inHg gauge pressure).
 Does the oven reach the specified minimum vacuum level, as indicated by the Set Vacuum display?

CO2 Display Functionality

Note: The concentration will initially read 0. Two (2) minutes may be required for a concentration to build up sufficient for detection.

  • Inspect the gas concentration display labeled "Set CO2%" on the unit. It should show a CO2 concentration for the .
 Does the CO2 Display show a concentration percentage?

CO2 Gas Delivery The unit must achieve a 5% gas concentration in the chamber. Allow a minimum of 5 minutes. Does the CO2 display show a 5% CO2 concentration after 5 minutes?
Yes No
   
CO2 Display Accuracy
  • Attach the digital gas analyzer sample tubing to the CO2 Sample Port located on the top left side of the unit.
  • Take a sample reading. If the unit CO2 display and the analyzer reading match, check Yes in the outcome block.
  • If there is a difference between the display and the gas analyzer readings, perform a calibration using the Calibrate the CO2 Display procedure in the Maintenance chapter of the user manual. A successful calibration should be noted as a Pass with Comment in the Results.
 Do the unit CO2 display and gas analyzer readings match?
